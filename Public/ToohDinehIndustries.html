Test Engineering Technician II

Confers with engineers, other technicians, production personnel and others regarding testing procedures and results and to resolve problems. Write computer programs to control device and electronic component test equipment, and testing specifications. Write, explain and demonstrate procedures on testing equipment or processes. Provide strategic plans for quality improvements based on test data. Use test instrumentation and in-circuit test equipment to perform problem analysis. Support production test equipment, including troubleshooting and repair of equipment failures, calibration, perform preventative maintenance, and maintain comprehensive equipment maintenance logs.
